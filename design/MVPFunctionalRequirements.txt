FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

As a registered user, I want to update my memories by editing or adding new content to them, ensuring that my timeline remains up-to-date and comprehensive.
As a registered user, I want to delete my memories that are no longer relevant or that I no longer wish to include in my timeline, keeping it organized and clutter-free.
As a registered user, I want my memories to be secure and protected with advanced security measures, ensuring that they remain safe and inaccessible to unauthorized users.
As a user, I want to search for specific memories by date, tags, or contributors, making it easier to find and relive memories.
